Welcome to Sassybyte.dev. This is my home portfolio page I created to showcase my current, previous and future projects. Sassybyte was created as my developer handle and hopefully my own company one day. I started coding early in the 2000's at ITT Tech. Shortly after 9/11 with all that was going on and the economy crashing, I would up dropping out in my 3rd semester because I had to go back to work full time, and put my edcuation on hold. I have gone down a few career paths since then, including customer service and I have been working as an insurance agent since 2016. As you can see alot of my inspiration comes from my 2 brilliant children Emily (7) and Bryan (13). You must notice alot of Chinchilla references so it's no secret my love for the 4 legged hopping fluff balls. Even the name Sassybyte was insired by one of my late but still loved fur babies named Sassy, who really loved to bite. And thats how Sassybyte developing and design came to be. I picked up coding again shortly after my little fluff passed away, to keep my mind off of things, and i started with HTML to CSS and am now learning javascript. I started with freeCodeCamp free learning and so far I have earned my responsive web certification. I am going to take an assessment here in the next few weeks for an apprentice program to enhance my skills and hopefully find my place in the industry. I will keep expanding and growing my portfolio as I learn, and if you are reading this then thank you for viewing my work.
