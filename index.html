Welcome to Sassybyte.dev. This is personal web page I created to showcase my current, previous and future projects. Alot of my inspiration comes from my chinchillas so you may see a lot of references in my project work.

I started with HTML to CSS and am now learning Javascript. I started with freeCodeCamp free learning and so far I have earned my responsive web certification. I am working through the Full Stack developer program currently and trying to get my work out there in the developer world. I am also prepping for an assessment here in the next few weeks for an apprentice program to enhance my skills and hopefully find my place in the industry. I will keep expanding and growing my portfolio as I learn, and if you are reading this then thank you for viewing my work.

Emily's Milkshakes- a freeCodeCamp certification project, based off a fictional menu by a startup business hosted by a little girl.
